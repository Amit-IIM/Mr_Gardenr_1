The Power of Biophilic Workspaces

Modern workspaces are moving beyond concrete walls. At MrGardenr, we bring biophilic design inside the office. Incorporating active living plants, customized green partition layouts, and moss walls has been scientifically proven to boost employee cognitive productivity by up to 15%.

We design, engineer, install, and completely maintain indoor landscape setups for large IT tech parks, startup hubs, premium co-working lounges, and grand corporate lobbies.

Our solutions focus on low-light indoor flora, allergen-free natural moss panels, smart indoor spot-lighting, and mess-free sub-irrigation planters that require zero manual daily watering.
